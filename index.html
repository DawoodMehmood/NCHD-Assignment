Latest News

September 20, 2022 News

  • NCHD established National Literacy Resource Center in collaboration with UNESCO.
  • The objective of NLRC is to conduct research on various eduational indicators and to dig out the ways to improve the rate of literacy in Pakistan.

September 20, 2022 News

  • NCHD has launched a book club with a clear intention to incorporate and rejuvinate the habits of reading among the learned class mostly over powerd by the technologicall inventions.
  • The NCHD book club was officially inaugurated by the Honorable Chairman NCHD Col.(R.) Dr. Amirullah Marwat.

September 21, 2022 News

  • NCHD Literacy Department has produced video lessons of the literacy Syllabus as per approved 2007 curriculum of the Govt. of Pakistan as a guideline for teaching of literacy professionals, Field Officer, Local Area Supervisors and illiterate learners in collaboration with UNESCO.
  • The Worthy Chairman, NCHD graced the occasion and inaugurated the lessons for Social Media for world wide online access at the NCHD Head Office.
  • The online Literacy course is expected to be used for the recap of lesson learnt by 4.3 Million Neo-literates of NCHD in the country.

September 21, 2022 News

  • The Honorable Parliamentary Secretary for Ministry of Federal Education and Professional Training Ms. Zeb Jaffar Paid a courtesy visit to the National Commission for Human Development.
  • The Honorable Chairman NCHD Col Retd Dr. Amirullah Marwat warmly welcomed the Honorable Guest and thanked her for the visit.
  • She was briefed about the NCHD work in the recent flash floods and informed Ms. Zeb Jaffar about NCHD rescue and relief services rendered to the flood victims in 65 districts of Pakistan.

September 14, 2022 News

  • Mr. Sam Wilson (ED TECH & DISTANCE LEARNING STRATEGIST, ODI FELLOW), paid a courtesy visit to the National Commission for Human Development.
  • He was received by the Honorable DG(LAC) and had the honor to call on Honorable Chairman NCHD.
  • During his visit, he was briefed about NCHD and its social development services in the field of Education, School Health, Literacy and Skill development.
  • The meeting was chaired by the Honorable chairman wherein, Mr. Sim Wilson was informed about the tremendous achievements that NCHD made during the past two decades.
  • He commended the efforts of NCHD in improving the Human Development Indicators in Pakistan and appreciated the Rapid Response by NCHD in the recent flash floods through providing rescue and relief services in all the affected districts of Pakistan because of its geographical presence and trained Human Resource.

September 14, 2022 News

  • NCHD signed an agreement with UNICEF to carry out feasibility study that will provide and in-depth analysis on the requirement of KP-EIP in 20 districts of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

September 02, 2022 News

  • NCHD launched project " Leave No Girls behind" funded by ACTED PAKISTAN" in Lakki Marwat.

December 21, 2021 News

  • Chairman National Commission for Human Col. (R). Dr. Amirullah Marwat accompanied by Director General NCHD, Mr. Hassan Baig presenting the Annual Report of the NCHD to President of Pakistan Dr. Arif Alvi at Aiwan-e-Sadr.

December 15, 2021 News

  • A kick off ceremony held at Margallah Hotel was attended by the partner organizations ( ACTED, RTP, ABES and NCHD).
  • The objective of the event was to give a detailed over view of the project named, 'Leave No Girls Behind'" (LNGB) by ACTED to update the stakeholders for smooth implementation of the project in District Lakki Marwat KP.
